Welcome to Aurora Beauty

Aurora Skincare & Beauty Therapy was created 6 years ago by Melissa Bland, who also works full time as a skincare & beauty specialist in the salon. Recently Melissa has expanded the salon to allow a variety of treatments to work along side her.

Each member of the team here is self employed, but collectively we strive to bring clients the highest quality treatment, products and professional service.

About Us

Our therapists and education team are at the heart of our mission to provide you with natural but powerful skincare along with our professional expertise to ensure you can truly achieve your ideal skin goals.
